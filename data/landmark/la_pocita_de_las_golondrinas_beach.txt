is a small public and natural saltwater beach in <a href="/wiki/Isabela,_Puerto_Rico" title="Isabela, Puerto Rico">Isabela</a>. It is safe for families with children because of its shallow waters (about two <a href="/wiki/Foot_(unit)" title="Foot (unit)">feet</a> deep) and lacks entries to the open sea. It is located about five minutes to the right of <a href="/w/index.php?title=Montones_Beach&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Montones Beach (page does not exist)">Montones Beach</a>. Currently this beach does not have facilities.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Still, it is surrounded by food kiosks and there are parking lots in walking distance.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Location">Location</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=La_Pocita_de_las_Golondrinas_Beach&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>It may be reached using PR-466 street in <a href="/wiki/Isabela,_Puerto_Rico" title="Isabela, Puerto Rico">Isabela</a>.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="What_can_be_done">What can be done</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=La_Pocita_de_las_Golondrinas_Beach&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: What can be done"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<ul><li><b>Swimming</b> Limited shallow waters allow a safe environment for swimmers and non swimmers alike. Water is warm, only about two feet deep.<sup id="cite_ref-islandsofpuertorico.com_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-islandsofpuertorico.com-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup></li>\n<li><b>Sun Bathing</b>: Excellent conditions year round.</li>\n<li><b>Snorkeling</b>: There are reefs, urchins, many fish and other marine wildlife.</li>\n<li><b>Sunset</b>: Since located on west side of <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a> the possibilities are greater of an amazing sunset.</li>\n<li><b>Hotels</b>: There are hotels nearby.</li></ul>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Facilities">Facilities</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=La_Pocita_de_las_Golondrinas_Beach&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Facilities"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<ul><li><b>Food</b>: There are kiosks nearby with traditional fried foods such as pastelillos / empanadillas.</li>\n<li><b>Lifeguards</b>: No Lifeguards available.<sup id="cite_ref-islandsofpuertorico.com_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-islandsofpuertorico.com-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup></li>\n<li><b>Bathroom facilities</b>: There are no bathroom facilities at the beach, but across the street there are restaurants and kiosks with restrooms.</li>\n<li><b>Parking</b>: There is a parking lot in walking distance.
