The <a href="/wiki/Arena" title="Arena">arena</a> was the only <a href="/wiki/Ice_skating" title="Ice skating">ice skating</a> facility in the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a> region. The facility is owned and administered by the city.
