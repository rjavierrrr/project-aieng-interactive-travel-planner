known as <b>Parque Lineal de Ponce</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> <b>Veredas del Labrador</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> or just <b>Parque Lineal</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Passive_park" class="mw-redirect" title="Passive park">passive park</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ponce,_Puerto_Rico" title="Ponce, Puerto Rico">Ponce, Puerto Rico</a>.  The park links to,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> but it is different from, a neighboring park also currently under construction called <i><a href="/wiki/Parque_Ecol%C3%B3gico_Urbano" class="mw-redirect" title="Parque Ecol\xc3\xb3gico Urbano">Parque Ecol\xc3\xb3gico Urbano</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The park runs along the <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Portugu%C3%A9s" class="mw-redirect" title="R\xc3\xado Portugu\xc3\xa9s">R\xc3\xado Portugu\xc3\xa9s</a> and <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Bucan%C3%A1" class="mw-redirect" title="R\xc3\xado Bucan\xc3\xa1">R\xc3\xado Bucan\xc3\xa1</a> rivers in the city of Ponce.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Construction">Construction <span class="anchor" id=""></span></h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parque_Lineal_Veredas_del_Labrador&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Construction"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Funds for the park had already been allocated by the administration of <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Cordero_Santiago" title="Rafael Cordero Santiago">Rafael Cordero Santiago</a> in the summer of 1991.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>9<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The park is being built as part of the <i>Ponce en Marcha</i> plan. Construction started under the administration of mayor <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Zayas_Seijo" title="Francisco Zayas Seijo">Francisco Zayas Seijo</a> (2005-2009) when an injection of $18 million was received from the Government of Puerto Rico as a result of a 1998 Supreme Court decision, during the administration of mayor <a href="/wiki/Churumba" class="mw-redirect" title="Churumba">Churumba</a>, which mandated the Government pay for infrastructure improvements in Ponce.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>10<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>11<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>12<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>13<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>14<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>15<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Phases">Phases</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parque_Lineal_Veredas_del_Labrador&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Phases"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The park consists of three main trenches built in three corresponding phases. Phase I runs for 3.8&#160;km along the eastern bank of <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Portugu%C3%A9s" class="mw-redirect" title="R\xc3\xado Portugu\xc3\xa9s">R\xc3\xado Portugu\xc3\xa9s</a> and along the western bank of <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Bucan%C3%A1" class="mw-redirect" title="R\xc3\xado Bucan\xc3\xa1">R\xc3\xado Bucan\xc3\xa1</a> bordering the <a href="/wiki/Julio_Enrique_Monagas_Family_Park" class="mw-redirect" title="Julio Enrique Monagas Family Park">Julio Enrique Monagas Family Park</a>. Phase II runs for 4.9&#160;km along the western bank of Rio Portugues to the point where it feeds Rio Bucana, whence it runs along its left bank to the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">Caribbean Sea</a>. Phase III runs parallel for 4&#160;km along the eastern bank of R\xc3\xado Bucana. Phase I was completed in October 2011, and Phase II in May 2012<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>16<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> at a cost of $38.1 million.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Description">Description</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parque_Lineal_Veredas_del_Labrador&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Description"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The park runs in a general north-south axis about 500 meters east of the center of the city. It makes its way through areas next to some of the city\'s landmarks, including <a href="/wiki/Campo_Atl%C3%A9tico_Charles_H._Terry" title="Campo Atl\xc3\xa9tico Charles H. Terry">Campo Atl\xc3\xa9tico Charles H. Terry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Centro_de_Convenciones_de_Ponce" class="mw-redirect" title="Centro de Convenciones de Ponce">Centro de Convenciones de Ponce</a> (Complejo Ferial de Puerto Rico), and <a href="/wiki/Parque_Familiar_Julio_Enrique_Monagas" title="Parque Familiar Julio Enrique Monagas">Parque Familiar Julio Enrique Monagas</a>. It also brushes by the musical centers of the city - the residential communities of B\xc3\xa9lgica and <a href="/wiki/San_Ant%C3%B3n" title="San Ant\xc3\xb3n">San Ant\xc3\xb3n</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>17<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Cost">Cost</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parque_Lineal_Veredas_del_Labrador&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Cost"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The entire project was estimated to cost $22 million<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>18<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  to build, with Phase I costing $4.5 million.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>19<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  As of October 2011, phases II and III were being built at a cost of $18 million.<sup
