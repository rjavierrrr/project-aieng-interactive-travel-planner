de Puerto Rico">Roman Catholic Archdiocese of San Juan de Puerto Rico</a></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Academia Interamericana Metro</b> (before <b> Academia del Sagrado Coraz\xc3\xb3n</b>) was founded in 1928 as a private and religious school in <a href="/wiki/Santurce,_San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="Santurce, San Juan, Puerto Rico">Santurce</a>, Puerto Rico by <a href="/wiki/Inter_American_University_of_Puerto_Rico" class="mw-redirect" title="Inter American University of Puerto Rico">Inter American University of Puerto Rico</a>- Metro Cumpus.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  The school provides elementary and high school education and is located in <a href="/wiki/Avenida_Juan_Ponce_de_Le%C3%B3n" title="Avenida Juan Ponce de Le\xc3\xb3n">Avenida Ponce de Le\xc3\xb3n</a>, at the Parada 19 in Santurce.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Before_foundation">Before foundation</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Before the school was founded, a small school affiliated with the church had existed on the current parking lot grounds of the church.  This early school was housed in a wooden structure that was destroyed when <a href="/wiki/1928_Okeechobee_hurricane" title="1928 Okeechobee hurricane">Hurricane Philip</a> struck Puerto Rico in 1928.
