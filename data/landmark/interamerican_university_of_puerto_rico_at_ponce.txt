It was founded in 1962. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Universidad_Interamericana_de_Puerto_Rico" class="mw-redirect" title="Universidad Interamericana de Puerto Rico">Universidad Interamericana de Puerto Rico</a> system, whose main campus is in <a href="/wiki/San_German" class="mw-redirect" title="San German">San German</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico_at_Ponce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The school opened in August 1962 as an extension program of the San German campus. It opened at facilities on Calle Villa, near <a href="/wiki/Ponce_Historic_Zone" title="Ponce Historic Zone">downtown Ponce</a>, at <a href="/wiki/Primera_Iglesia_Metodista_Unida_de_Ponce" title="Primera Iglesia Metodista Unida de Ponce">Primera Iglesia Metodista La Resurrecci\xc3\xb3n</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The school then moved to calle Marina and Calle Jobos, at the locale also known as Casa Forneris.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In 1966, more spacious quarters were established at the intersection of Calle Castillo and Calle Mayor.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>a<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In the early 1980s the university started to build a new campus east of the city. In 1984 it moved to this new campus in the Mercedita sector of Barrio <a href="/wiki/Vayas" class="mw-redirect" title="Vayas">Vayas</a>, Ponce. In 1992 the school became an autonomous campus of the Inter-American University system.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Academics">Academics</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico_at_Ponce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Academics"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The school offers programs in education, health, and management. It has over 25 academic programs, including offering five <a href="/wiki/Master_Degree" class="mw-redirect" title="Master Degree">Master Degrees</a> in Education, Business and Criminal Justice. It also offers 22 <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_Degree" class="mw-redirect" title="Bachelor Degree">Bachelor Degrees</a>, seven <a href="/wiki/Associate_Degree" class="mw-redirect" title="Associate Degree">Associate Degrees</a>, and four technical certificates. It has an enrollment of approximately 5,000 students.  The university offers academic programs in 11 teaching units.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Expansion">Expansion</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico_at_Ponce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Expansion"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>In August 2012, the school announced a $10 million expansion plan that would create a college preparatory school. The new facility is scheduled to open in August 2013 in the immediacy of the current school grounds.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Notes">Notes</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span
