in Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico</div>\n<p>The <b>Centro Cultural Baudilio Vega Berr\xc3\xados</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Cultural_center" title="Cultural center">cultural center</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Mayag%C3%BCez,_Puerto_Rico" title="Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico">Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico</a>.\nLocated in the heart of the city, aside the <a href="/wiki/Mayag%C3%BCez_City_Hall" title="Mayag\xc3\xbcez City Hall">City Hall</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Teatro_Yag%C3%BCez" title="Teatro Yag\xc3\xbcez">Teatro Yag\xc3\xbcez</a>, extending between <a href="/wiki/Calle_de_la_Candelaria_(Mayag%C3%BCez)" class="mw-redirect" title="Calle de la Candelaria (Mayag\xc3\xbcez)">Candelaria Street (former <i>McKinley</i>)</a> at front to the <a href="/wiki/Ernesto_Ramos_Antonini" title="Ernesto Ramos Antonini">Ernesto Ramos Antonini</a> Street (former <i>Eleven of August</i>) at back.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Architecture">Architecture</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Centro_Cultural_Baudilio_Vega_Berr%C3%ADos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Architecture"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The Cultural Center of Mayag\xc3\xbcez is a two-story building with a facade that is dominated by arches, built at a cost of one million dollars under the management of <a href="/wiki/Baudilio_Vega_Berr%C3%ADos" title="Baudilio Vega Berr\xc3\xados">Baudilio Vega Berr\xc3\xados</a>, for whom the building was named. It was built strictly with municipal funds.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It has a theater with capacity for seven hundred people, a library, a history museum, reading and conference rooms as well as a modern cafeteria.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Centro_Cultural_Baudilio_Vega_Berr%C3%ADos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The Centro Cultural Baudilio Vega Berr\xc3\xados is located in place that for many years occupied the town fire station.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The Cultural Center began as an organization founded in 1958 under the presidency of <a href="/w/index.php?title=Amador_Ram%C3%ADrez_Silva&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Amador Ram\xc3\xadrez Silva (page does not exist)">Amador Ram\xc3\xadrez Silva</a> as part of a project of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Institute_of_Culture&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Puerto Rico Institute of Culture (page does not exist)">Puerto Rico Institute of Culture</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Aguilo_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Aguilo-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  With the cooperation and donations of different institutions and people from Mayaguez, the Center starts to become a reality with a series of activities.  Since then the Municipal Administration has become an important column in the organization.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2017)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>\n</p><p>With the city\'s bicentennial approaching in 1960, the Bicentennial Committee proposed the construction of a building that would house the different facets of Mayag\xc3\xbcez cultural life.<sup id="cite_ref-Aguilo_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Aguilo-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  In 1968, the Cultural Center was finally inaugurated.<sup id="cite_ref-Aguilo_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Aguilo-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>Don Baudilio Vega Berr\xc3\xados, <a href="/wiki/List_of_mayors_of_Mayag%C3%BCez,_Puerto_Rico" title="List of mayors of Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico">Mayor of the city</a>, said during its inauguration, that: "... in fact, at all classes social-working, artisans, dependents on commerce, businessmen, industrialists, it was notable the concern towards the joy in their cultural recreational field as so many jobs in their daily lives".<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Therefore, the building was constructed for the Cultural Center of the city of Mayag\xc3\xbcez aiming to replace the old municipal theater using modern construction techniques.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>On September 3, 1983 being mayor of the city <a href="/wiki/Benjamin_Cole_(mayor)" class="mw-redirect" title="Benjamin Cole (mayor)">Benjamin Cole</a>, the Cultural Center was dedicated in honor of Baudilio Vega.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\nCurrently, the Cultural Center of Mayag\xc3\xbcez houses a public library, cultural theater and tourist office as well as the Municipal <a href="/wiki/Bomba_(Puerto_Rico)" title="Bomba (Puerto Rico)">Bomba</a> School.<sup id="cite_ref-Mango_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Mango-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="References">References</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span
