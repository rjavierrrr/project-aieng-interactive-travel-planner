title="Special:EditPage/Template:Sidebar Tourism in Puerto Rico"><abbr title="Edit this template">e</abbr></a></li></ul></div></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Dr. Pedro Rossell\xc3\xb3 Gonz\xc3\xa1lez Puerto Rico Convention Center</b> (<b>PRCC</b>) (or <i>Centro de Convenciones de Puerto Rico Dr. Pedro Rossell\xc3\xb3 Gonz\xc3\xa1lez</i>  in Spanish),<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> or simply <b>Puerto Rico Convention Center</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Convention_center" title="Convention center">convention center</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Isla_Grande_(Santurce)" title="Isla Grande (Santurce)">Isla Grande</a> (recently named the <a href="/wiki/Isla_Grande_(Santurce)" title="Isla Grande (Santurce)">Convention Center District</a>), in <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan, Puerto Rico</a>  owned by the Puerto Rico Convention Center District Authority, a <a href="/wiki/Government_agency_of_Puerto_Rico" class="mw-redirect" title="Government agency of Puerto Rico">government agency of Puerto Rico</a>, and managed by ASM Global <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://www.asmglobal.com/">[1]</a>. With a total space of 600,000-square-foot (55,742 m2)<a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://prconvention.com/en/eventplanners/#amenities">[2]</a> and designed by <a href="/wiki/Tvsdesign" title="Tvsdesign">tvsdesign</a>, it is the largest convention center in the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a> and one of the most technologically advanced in the <a href="/wiki/Americas" title="Americas">Americas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>The Convention Center includes a 152,700-square-foot (14,190&#160;m<sup>2</sup>) Exhibition Hall that can seat up to 16,965 people and a 39,500-square-foot (3,670&#160;m<sup>2</sup>) ballroom that can accommodate up to 4,158 people.  All in all, approximately 30,000 people can be accommodated within the center simultaneously and can handle conventions of organizations as large as 10,000 participants.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The current PRCC general manager is Jorge P\xc3\xa9rez.\n</p><p>The center is operated by the <i>Puerto Rico Convention Center District Authority</i>, which also operates the <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Miguel_Agrelot_Coliseum" title="Jos\xc3\xa9 Miguel Agrelot Coliseum">Jos\xc3\xa9 Miguel Agrelot Coliseum</a>, also known as the "Choliseo", and the Government Reception Center, known as the <a href="/wiki/Antiguo_Casino_de_Puerto_Rico" title="Antiguo Casino de Puerto Rico">Antiguo Casino de Puerto Rico</a>, in Old San Juan, as well as the 113-acre district on which the Convention Center and the attached Sheraton hotel are located.  Its current executive director is Omar Marrero.\n</p><p>The convention center is named after former Governor <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Rossell%C3%B3" title="Pedro Rossell\xc3\xb3">Pedro Rossell\xc3\xb3</a>, who proposed and initiated the design, finance and construction of the structure.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>After the hit from <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Maria" title="Hurricane Maria">Hurricane Maria</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2017_Atlantic_hurricane_season" title="2017 Atlantic hurricane season">2017 Atlantic hurricane season</a>, events at the Puerto Rico Convention Center were cancelled from mid-September through the end of the year while the facility was used as a warehouse and collection center by the Puerto Rican Government to provide food, water and basic necessities to those affected by the deadliest and costliest hurricane in Puerto Rican history. The Convention Center re-opened in early 2018 and has been hosting a wide range of events since. Numerous improvements, such as <a href="/wiki/Distrito_T-Mobile" title="Distrito T-Mobile">Distrito T-Mobile</a>, have been made around the convention center area as well, which offers hotels, restaurants, casinos and other amenities.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>1994 - Governor Rossell\xc3\xb3 and <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico_Tourism_Company" title="Puerto Rico Tourism Company">Puerto Rico Tourism Company</a> Executive Director <a href="/wiki/Luis_Fortu%C3%B1o" title="Luis Fortu\xc3\xb1o">Luis Fortu\xc3\xb1o</a> announce the development of the Golden Triangle Project to be located in the Miramar section of Santurce, the old Isla Grande naval station and the Puerta de Tierra section of San Juan.\n</p><p>1996 - Design of new Convention Center commences\n</p><p>1997 - Three-year long relocation and razing of state government facilities at old Isla Grande naval station begins\n</p><p>2000 - Groundbreaking ceremony held and construction begins\n</p><p>2001 - Puerto Rico Convention Center District Authority begins operations\n</p><p>2005 - Gov. <a href="/wiki/An%C3%ADbal_Acevedo_Vil%C3%A1" title="An\xc3\xadbal Acevedo Vil\xc3\xa1">An\xc3\xadbal Acevedo Vil\xc3\xa1</a> inaugurates Convention Center\n</p><p>2009 - Convention Center achieves 1.5 million visitors in 1,500 Events\n</p><p>2009 - Sheraton\'s anchor Hotel at the Convention District initiates operations\n</p><p>2010 - Gov. <a href="/wiki/Luis_Fortu%C3%B1o" title="Luis Fortu\xc3\xb1o">Luis Fortu\xc3\xb1o</a> signs law naming center after his predecessor, Gov. Rossell\xc3\xb3, and approves plan for <a href="/w/index.php?title=District_Live&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="District Live (page does not exist)">District Live</a>, an entertainment complex next to the center.\n</p><p>2015 - The PRCC celebrates its tenth anniversary<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> and 4.7 million visitors.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>2021 - Gov. <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Pierluisi" title="Pedro Pierluisi">Pedro Pierluisi</a> inaugurates <a href="/wiki/Distrito_T-Mobile" title="Distrito T-Mobile">Distrito T-Mobile</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Planning_and_construction">Planning and construction</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Planning and construction"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<figure class="mw-halign-right" typeof="mw:File/Thumb"><a href="/wiki/File:PRCC_3.JPG" class="mw-file-description"><img src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c8/PRCC_3.JPG/250px-PRCC_3.JPG" decoding="async" width="250" height="188" class="mw-file-element" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c8/PRCC_3.JPG/375px-PRCC_3.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c8/PRCC_3.JPG/500px-PRCC_3.JPG 2x" data-file-width="2304" data-file-height="1728" /></a><figcaption>The PRCC main entrance</figcaption></figure>\n<p>The Convention Center\'s design was inspired by the ocean. It has a signature roof imitating a wave, and the ocean theme continues throughout the facility, including details like its custom designed carpets and door pulls.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Location">Location</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Location"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The PRCC is located in the San Juan peninsula known as the <a href="/wiki/Isla_Grande_(Santurce)" title="Isla Grande (Santurce)">Isla Grande</a> in what used to be a US Naval Base in Isla Grande. The PRCC is located next to the <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Luis_Ribas_Dominicci_Airport" title="Fernando Luis Ribas Dominicci Airport">Isla Grande Airport</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Port_of_San_Juan" title="Port of San Juan">Port of San Juan</a> Pan American Pier.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="The_PRCC_District">The PRCC District</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: The PRCC District"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The PRCC is part of a planned <style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1238216509">.mw-parser-output .vanchor>:target~.vanchor-text{background-color:#b1d2ff}@media screen{html.skin-theme-clientpref-night .mw-parser-output .vanchor>:target~.vanchor-text{background-color:#0f4dc9}}@media screen and (prefers-color-scheme:dark){html.skin-theme-clientpref-os .mw-parser-output .vanchor>:target~.vanchor-text{background-color:#0f4dc9}}</style><span class="vanchor"><span id="Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center_District"></span><span class="vanchor-text">Puerto Rico Convention Center District</span></span> (master-planned by <a href="/wiki/Sasaki_Associates" class="mw-redirect" title="Sasaki Associates">Sasaki Associates</a>). The main project is the <a href="/wiki/Aloft_Hotels" title="Aloft Hotels">Aloft hotel</a> with 177 rooms, and a music hall which can seat 6,000 visitors, as well as a <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Cinemas" title="Caribbean Cinemas">Caribbean Cinemas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-for_business_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-for_business-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The Puerto Rican government selected <a href="/wiki/Starwood_Hotels_and_Resorts" title="Starwood Hotels and Resorts">Starwood</a>, under its Sheraton flag, to operate the reported $209 million facility, known as the Sheraton Puerto Rico Hotel &amp; Casino.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>9<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> With these two hotels and others in the area, the district now has 1,762 hotel rooms available for visitors.<sup id="cite_ref-hotel_business_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hotel_business-10"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>10<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>In recent years a number of projects have been under construction, including several restaurants and bars, an entertainment event venue known as <a href="/wiki/Distrito_T-Mobile" title="Distrito T-Mobile">Distrito T-Mobile</a>, a container park and the EcoExploratorio (an interactive museum and aquarium).<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>11<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>12<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Additionally, the Puerto Rico Film District composed by film studios and other features, including a university, is being built within the zone.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>13<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Facilities_and_services">Facilities and services</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Convention_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Facilities and services"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The convention center is able to accommodate groups of up to 30,000<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>14<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> people with a total space of 600,000 square feet (56,000&#160;m<sup>2</sup>)<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>15<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> and 152,700 square feet (14,190&#160;m<sup>2</sup>)<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>16<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> of exhibit space subdivided into three halls, all on one level. The center has adjacent parking lots with a total capacity of 2,215 vehicles,<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>17<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> although this may be increased in the future.\n</p><p>The facility can provide <a href="/wiki/Broadband" title="Broadband">broadband</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wireless_internet" class="mw-redirect" title="Wireless internet">wireless internet</a> connectivity, video conferencing, and simultaneous language translations, among others throughout the center. All meeting rooms and ballrooms are equipped with <a href="/wiki/Ethernet" title="Ethernet">Ethernet</a> connection and capabilities. It also has a business center which assists business guests and attendees by providing computer and typewriter rental, printing, secretarial service, copying, fax transmission and receipt, package shipping, office products, and other services. A central security office is located within the center. The center also has inhouse catering, concessions and cleaning services for any and all activities.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Gallery">Gallery</h2><span
