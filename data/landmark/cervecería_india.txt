href="http://www.cerveceradepr.com/">www<wbr />.cerveceradepr<wbr />.com</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Compa\xc3\xb1\xc3\xada Cervecera de Puerto Rico</b> (formerly known as <b>Cervecer\xc3\xada India</b>) is a large <a href="/wiki/Brewery" title="Brewery">brewery</a> in <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It is located in <a href="/wiki/Mayag%C3%BCez,_Puerto_Rico" title="Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico">Mayag\xc3\xbcez</a>, Puerto Rico. The company\'s main brand is <i>Medalla Light</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Compa%C3%B1%C3%ADa_Cervecera_de_Puerto_Rico&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The company was founded on November 2, 1937, by the brothers Vald\xc3\xa9s Cobi\xc3\xa1n: <a href="/wiki/Alfonso_Vald%C3%A9s_Cobi%C3%A1n" title="Alfonso Vald\xc3\xa9s Cobi\xc3\xa1n">Alfonso</a>, Sabino and <a href="/wiki/Ram%C3%B3n_Vald%C3%A9s_Cobi%C3%A1n" title="Ram\xc3\xb3n Vald\xc3\xa9s Cobi\xc3\xa1n">Ram\xc3\xb3n Vald\xc3\xa9s Cobi\xc3\xa1n</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Mayag%C3%BCez,_Puerto_Rico" title="Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico">Mayag\xc3\xbcez</a>, the largest city on the west coast of <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>. The Vald\xc3\xa9s brothers were following the example of their father, Don Ramon Vald\xc3\xa9s, who had founded the Mayag\xc3\xbcez Light, Power and Ice Company in 1910. In 1938, their first beer, Cerveza India, was released to the public. The company competed with two other native beer breweries, "Real" and "Cerveceria Corona" (Cerveceria Corona de Puerto Rico "subsequently sold its trademark rights to Cervecer\xc3\xada Modelo de M\xc3\xa9xico, which then launched Cerveza Corona as Modelo\'s Corona Extra)."<sup id="cite_ref-Historia_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Historia-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Alfonso_Vald%C3%A9s_Cobi%C3%A1n" title="Alfonso Vald\xc3\xa9s Cobi\xc3\xa1n">Alfonso</a>, as chairman and president, successfully led his company converting it into Puerto Rico\'s largest beer brewery and manufacturer of canned and bottled soft drinks.\n</p>\n<figure class="mw-default-size" northbound.</figcaption></figure>\n<p>In the late 70s, the brewery was forced to expand its portfolio and produce a lighter beer with less calories than their Cerveza India. Until that time, India was the only alcoholic beverage producing plant in Mayag\xc3\xbcez.\n</p><p>In 1985, their main competition, Corona, went bankrupt and left a free way to Cervecer\xc3\xada India, which was in full swing after the release of its new product Medalla Light years before.<sup id="cite_ref-Historia_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Historia-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In October 2009, the brewery reverted its name to "Compa\xc3\xb1\xc3\xada Cervecera de Puerto Rico".\n</p><p>From 2002 to 2017, Camalia Vald\xc3\xa9s served as president and CEO of the company.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  In 2017, Grace Marie Vald\xc3\xa9s succeeded Camalia as president and CEO.<sup id="cite_ref-ENDI_2017_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ENDI_2017-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>The company works to promote the art and culture of Puerto Rico.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It soon later launched an artisanal brewery, <i>Del Oeste Brewery</i> within its main brewing facility.\n</p><p>In 2018, the company announced a distribution agreement to expand the Medalla Light brand into the United States, specifically Florida. <sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Products">Products</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Compa%C3%B1%C3%ADa_Cervecera_de_Puerto_Rico&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Products"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The company\'s main product is <i>Medalla Light</i>, a 4.2% <a href="/wiki/Abv" class="mw-redirect" title="Abv">abv</a> <a href="/wiki/Pale_lager" title="Pale lager">pale lager</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>9<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The brewery also produces <a href="/wiki/Malta_(soft_drink)" title="Malta (soft drink)">Malta India</a>, a non-alcoholic soft drink made from barley. In 1940, it began producing <a href="/wiki/Crush_(brand)" class="mw-redirect" title="Crush (brand)">Orange Crush</a> and Old Colony sodas. In May 2010, the brewery announced the launch of their new beer, Silver Key Light, which hit the market on June 1, 2010.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>10<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> They launched Magna, a premium beer, on August 1, 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>11<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> On November 5, 2019, Cerveza India, which was their first beer, was relaunched as a limited edition.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Beers">Beers</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Compa%C3%B1%C3%ADa_Cervecera_de_Puerto_Rico&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Beers"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<ul><li><i>Medalla Light</i> - <a href="/wiki/Pale_Lager" class="mw-redirect" title="Pale Lager">Pale Lager</a> with 4.2% abv.
