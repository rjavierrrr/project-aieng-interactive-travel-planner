href="/wiki/Ponce,_Puerto_Rico" title="Ponce, Puerto Rico">Ponce</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>. Inaugurated in 1931,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> it originally housed a movie house until 1980, from 1991 to 1998 it house a shopping mall, and stating in 2004 it housed a <a href="/wiki/Boutique_hotel" title="Boutique hotel">boutique hotel</a>. Its architecture is <a href="/wiki/Art_Deco" title="Art Deco">Art Deco</a>. Originally called <b>Teatro Delicias</b>, it was renamed Fox Delicias many years after its inauguration based on a contract with <a href="/wiki/20th_Century_Fox" class="mw-redirect" title="20th Century Fox">20th Century Fox</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Location">Location</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The historic structure is located across the historic <a href="/wiki/Plaza_Las_Delicias" title="Plaza Las Delicias">Plaza Las Delicias</a> in the heart of the <a href="/wiki/Ponce_Historic_Zone" title="Ponce Historic Zone">Ponce Historic Zone</a>. Its location just north of Plaza Las Delicias makes it part of barrio <a href="/wiki/Segundo_(Ponce)" class="mw-redirect" title="Segundo (Ponce)">Segundo</a>.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Design">Design</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Design"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The building was designed by <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Porrata-Doria" class="mw-redirect" title="Francisco Porrata-Doria">Francisco Porrata-Doria</a>, who also designed Hotel Melia, <a href="/wiki/Banco_de_Ponce_(building)" title="Banco de Ponce (building)">Banco de Ponce</a> and many other structures in Puerto Rico.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The building bears an Art Deco architectural structure with an impressive multi-level front facade.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Theater">Theater</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Theater"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The theater had its origins in the late 1920s when <a href="/wiki/Juan_Serrall%C3%A9s" class="mw-redirect" title="Juan Serrall\xc3\xa9s">Pedro Juan Serralles</a> purchased a piece of land in the center of the city to build the first <a href="/wiki/Movie_theater" title="Movie theater">movie theater</a> in Ponce. The structure was completed in 1931. The owners contracted with <a href="/wiki/Twentieth_Century_Fox" class="mw-redirect" title="Twentieth Century Fox">Twentieth Century Fox</a> for showing the movies.  Given the movie contract and the location of the theater, it was named Teatro Fox Delicias.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It closed its doors as a movie theater in 1980.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Mall">Mall</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Mall"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>After serving Ponce moviegoers for some five decades, the building was sold to the <a href="/wiki/Eduardo_Rubert%C3%A9_Bis%C3%B3" title="Eduardo Rubert\xc3\xa9 Bis\xc3\xb3">Rubert\xc3\xa9 family</a>. In 1989 the theater was converted into a mall under the design of architect Axel Bonilla.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It was named the Fox Delicias Mall.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The new mall opened in March, 1991. It had 26 commercial spaces ranging from 300 square feet (28&#160;m<sup>2</sup>) to 1,200 square feet (110&#160;m<sup>2</sup>). It also had a cafe-theater. The total space was 30,000 square feet (2,800&#160;m<sup>2</sup>). Its president is Eduardo Ruberte Huertas. Its renovation lasted a year and cost $3 million.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>9<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Hotel">Hotel</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Teatro_Fox_Delicias&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Hotel"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<figure class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File/Thumb"><a href="/wiki/File:Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce,_Puerto_Rico.jpg" class="mw-file-description"><img src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg/220px-Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="293" class="mw-file-element" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg/330px-Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg/440px-Teatro_Fox_Delicias_in_Ponce%2C_Puerto_Rico.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1200" data-file-height="1600" /></a><figcaption><span title="Spanish-language text"><i lang="es">Teatro Fox Delicias</i></span> redeveloped as a hotel</figcaption></figure>\n<p>In 2004 the building started conversion into a hotel.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>10<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>11<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The boutique hotel had 30 rooms. The new hotel opened in 2004.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>12<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> While it operated as a hotel, the structure continued to experience a flow of non-resident tourists.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>13<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It closed around 2010 and in 2018, Grupo Misla Villalba, a group of local investors, acquired the property and it\'s currently under remodelation to reopen as a Pop Art themed hotel named \xe2\x80\x9cThe Fox Hotel\xe2\x80\x9d.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>14<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>15<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>16<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The hotel reopened on 4 December 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>17<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Other_area_theaters">Other area theaters</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span
