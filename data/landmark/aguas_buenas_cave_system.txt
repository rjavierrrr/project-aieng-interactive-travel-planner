de Aguas Buenas</i>) since 2002, which also extends to the municipalities of <a href="/wiki/Caguas,_Puerto_Rico" title="Caguas, Puerto Rico">Caguas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cidra,_Puerto_Rico" title="Cidra, Puerto Rico">Cidra</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The reserve is important for the number of bat species found in the system, some of which are endangered (<i><a href="/wiki/Antillean_ghost-faced_bat" title="Antillean ghost-faced bat">Mormoops blanvillei</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Leach%27s_single_leaf_bat" title="Leach&#39;s single leaf bat">Monophyllus redmani</a></i> and <i><a href="/w/index.php?title=Pteronotus_portoricensis&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Pteronotus portoricensis (page does not exist)">Pteronotus portoricensis</a></i>).<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In addition to being an important bat preserve, the nature reserve protects an important hydrological basin which is the source of a number of rivers and creeks that form part of the <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Grande_de_Lo%C3%ADza" title="R\xc3\xado Grande de Lo\xc3\xadza">Lo\xc3\xadza River</a> basin.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Geology">Geology</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geology"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The cave system is found in one of the oldest <a href="/wiki/Karst" title="Karst">karst</a> zones in Puerto Rico.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Named_caves">Named caves</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Named caves"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Cueva_Oscura">Cueva Oscura</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cueva Oscura"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p><i>Cueva Oscura</i> (Spanish for "dark cave") is home to the two largest cave rooms in the system. This cave is also considered an important bat habitat and is home to the most diverse and abundant bat colonies in the system.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Cueva_Ermita">Cueva Ermita</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Cueva Ermita"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p><i>Cueva Ermita</i> (Spanish for "hermitage cave"), also known as <i>Cueva Nivel del Rio</i> ("river-level cave"), is the source of the <a href="/wiki/Cag%C3%BCitas_River" title="Cag\xc3\xbcitas River">Cag\xc3\xbcitas River</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Cueva_M\xc3\xbacara_and_Cueva_Geco"><span id="Cueva_M.C3.BAcara_and_Cueva_Geco"></span>Cueva M\xc3\xbacara and Cueva Geco</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Cueva M\xc3\xbacara and Cueva Geco"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p><i>Cueva M\xc3\xbacara</i> (<a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rican_Spanish" title="Puerto Rican Spanish">Puerto Rican Spanish</a> for "owl cave") is a steep cave and its entrance is home to a number of bat colonies. <i>Cueva Geco</i> is interconnected with <i>Cueva M\xc3\xbacara</i> and being mostly vertical, is the steepest cave in the system.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Cueva_Grillo_and_Cueva_Murci\xc3\xa9lago"><span id="Cueva_Grillo_and_Cueva_Murci.C3.A9lago"></span>Cueva Grillo and Cueva Murci\xc3\xa9lago</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Cueva Grillo and Cueva Murci\xc3\xa9lago"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p><i>Cueva Grillo</i> (Spanish for "cricket cave") used to be the largest cave in the system but after a partial collapse the cave is now separated from the now called <i>Cueva Murci\xc3\xa9lago</i> (Spanish for "bat cave") which used to be home to a large number of bats until <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Maria" title="Hurricane Maria">Hurricane Maria</a> in 2017 when the bat population greatly decreased in the cave.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> \n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Recreation">Recreation</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aguas_Buenas_Cave_System&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Recreation"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The caves currently have no infrastructure for visitors.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="See_also">See also</h2><span
