It has students from pre-kindergarten to the 12th grade.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Background">Background</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Saint John\'s School is a <a href="/wiki/Non-profit" class="mw-redirect" title="Non-profit">non-profit</a>, college preparatory, <a href="/wiki/Nonsectarian" title="Nonsectarian">nonsectarian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coeducational" class="mw-redirect" title="Coeducational">coeducational</a> day school founded in 1915.  The school has an enrollment of over 800 students from pre pre-<a href="/wiki/Kindergarten" title="Kindergarten">kindergarten</a> to grade twelve.  Instruction is mostly in English with the exception of language courses.  Technology in the classroom includes <a href="/wiki/Tablet_computer" title="Tablet computer">tablets</a>, <a href="/wiki/Laptop" title="Laptop">laptops</a> and <a href="/wiki/Smart_board" class="mw-redirect" title="Smart board">smart boards</a>.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Academics">Academics</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Academics"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Elementary_School_(Pre-Pre-Kinder_through_Grade_6)"><span id="Elementary_School_.28Pre-Pre-Kinder_through_Grade_6.29"></span>Elementary School (Pre-Pre-Kinder through Grade 6)</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Elementary School (Pre-Pre-Kinder through Grade 6)"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Traditional subjects such as reading and writing, mathematics, <a href="/wiki/Social_studies" title="Social studies">social studies</a>, and science are organized by themes.  The Spanish language program begins in PPK.  Spanish as a Second Language instruction also is offered.  Students have a <a href="/wiki/Computer_literacy" title="Computer literacy">computer literacy</a> program, a physical development program based on the concept of \xe2\x80\x9cwellness\xe2\x80\x9d, and an integrated arts program including art, music, and performing arts. <a href="/wiki/Community_service" title="Community service">Community service</a> projects are integrated into the academic programs.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Secondary_School_(Grades_7_through_12)"><span id="Secondary_School_.28Grades_7_through_12.29"></span>Secondary School (Grades 7 through 12)</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Secondary School (Grades 7 through 12)"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>For grades seven and eight, every student takes the core academic classes of English, <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">math</a>, social studies, science, and <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>.  <a href="/wiki/Physical_education" title="Physical education">Physical education</a> classes meet daily.  In addition, all students take a number of exploratory classes that include visual and <a href="/wiki/Performing_arts" title="Performing arts">performing arts</a>, technology, <a href="/wiki/Writing_Workshop_(Greaves,_et_al.)" class="mw-redirect" title="Writing Workshop (Greaves, et al.)">writing workshop</a>, and research.  <a href="/w/index.php?title=Algebra_I&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Algebra I (page does not exist)">Algebra I</a> is offered to all eighth graders with a simultaneous class in math enrichment for those who need extra support. Each grade does community service projects a few times a year.\n</p><p>Students in grades nine through twelve follow a <a href="/wiki/College_preparatory_course" title="College preparatory course">college preparatory</a> curriculum enriched by electives and <a href="/wiki/Advanced_placement" class="mw-redirect" title="Advanced placement">advanced placement</a> courses.  <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Placement" title="Advanced Placement">Advanced placement</a> courses are usually offered in <a href="/wiki/English_literature" title="English literature">English literature</a>, Spanish literature, <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French language</a>, biology, chemistry, physics, calculus, economics, psychology, human geography, United States history and European history. Elective courses usually offered include anatomy and physiology, contemporary communications, art, film analysis, French, <a href="/wiki/Hispanic_culture" class="mw-redirect" title="Hispanic culture">Hispanic culture</a>, French culture and journalism.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="AP_Courses_Offered">AP Courses Offered</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: AP Courses Offered"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading4"><h4 id="Humanities">Humanities</h4><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Humanities"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>AP Human Geography, AP Language and Composition, AP Psychology, AP European History, AP United States History, AP Government and Politics\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading4"><h4 id="STEM">STEM</h4><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: STEM"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>AP Physics I, AP Physics II, AP Chemistry, AP Biology, AP Computer Science A, AP Computer Science Principles, AP Statistics, AP Calculus AB, AP Calculus BC\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Advanced_non-AP_Courses">Advanced non-AP Courses</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Advanced non-AP Courses"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Multivariable Calculus (taken by only one person in 2015)\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Athletics">Athletics</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Athletics"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Saint John\'s is an active member of the LAMEPI league at the elementary level and the PRHSAA at the middle and high school level.  Mini, Juvenile, Junior Varsity and Varsity teams usually include girls\xe2\x80\x99 and boys\xe2\x80\x99 cross country, <a href="/wiki/Soccer" class="mw-redirect" title="Soccer">soccer</a>, volleyball, <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a>, swimming, tennis, and indoor soccer.  <a href="/wiki/Sailing" title="Sailing">Sailing</a> is offered to students from grades five through eight as an exploratory program.\n</p><p>As part of the intramural program, there is a <a href="/wiki/Volleyball" title="Volleyball">volleyball</a> and basketball infantile league which includes first through fourth grade girls\xe2\x80\x99 and boys\xe2\x80\x99.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Facilities">Facilities</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Facilities"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Saint John\'s includes an <a href="/wiki/Primary_school" title="Primary school">elementary school</a> complex with specialized areas for art, music, performing arts, computers and science, and a secondary building which contains science laboratories and a technology center.  The library facilities include separate areas for elementary and secondary students.  Each classroom is equipped with a computer and a monitor.  All math and science classrooms are also equipped with <a href="/wiki/Projector" title="Projector">projectors</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Smart_board" class="mw-redirect" title="Smart board">smart board</a>. The school\'s  <a href="/wiki/Gym" title="Gym">gymnasium</a> is used by all students.  The school cafeteria serves breakfast, lunch, and snacks each day.\n</p><p>Administrative and Business Offices are housed in Casa Marbella.  Admissions and Development Offices, as well as additional high school classrooms, can be found in The Annex.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="STEM_Extracurriculars">STEM Extracurriculars</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: STEM Extracurriculars"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="MathCounts">MathCounts</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: MathCounts"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The school has a club for <a href="/wiki/Middle_school" title="Middle school">middle schoolers</a> in grades 6\xe2\x80\x938 to participate annually in the <a href="/wiki/Mathcounts" title="Mathcounts">MATHCOUNTS</a> Competition. Weekly trainings take place on Thursdays with a coach and coach assistant to help out the mathletes\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Math_Bowl_Participation">Math Bowl Participation</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Math Bowl Participation"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>High School members grades 9th-12th annually compete in statewide team math bowl participations in schools around the island such as Marista, Seso, and Perpetuo Socorro usually with a team captain and 2 to 3 teammates. Mathletes train weekly together or individually for competitions that cover from Algebra II to Pre-algebra.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Science_Bowl">Science Bowl</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Science Bowl"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The SJS Science bowl consists of high schoolers from 9th to 12th grade who practice weekly on Thursday afternoons with buzzers. They annually compete in the <a href="/wiki/Mayag%C3%BCez,_Puerto_Rico" title="Mayag\xc3\xbcez, Puerto Rico">Mayaguez</a> Science Bowl in two teams, team A and B with a team captain for each to answer short answer questions. They also compete in the annual SESO Science bowl, in teams of 4, each with a team captain to direct the team members.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Other_Clubs_and_Activities">Other Clubs and Activities</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Other Clubs and Activities"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="School_Band">School Band</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: School Band"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The school teamed up with the Conservatory of Puerto Rico in 2015 to bring a school band consisting of <a href="/wiki/Flute" title="Flute">flute</a>, clarinet, <a href="/wiki/Saxophone" title="Saxophone">saxophones</a>, trombones, <a href="/wiki/Trumpet" title="Trumpet">trumpets</a>, piano, drums, <a href="/wiki/Electric_bass" class="mw-redirect" title="Electric bass">electric bass</a>, and guitar.\n</p><p>The band performs annually at the School\'s Noche Puertorriqueno (Puerto Rican Night) and at the end of the year.  They have performed at the Conservatory of Puerto Rico in December 2015.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="MIT_LaunchX_Club">MIT LaunchX Club</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_John%27s_School_(San_Juan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: MIT LaunchX Club"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Started in 2017, the club consists of student entrepreneurs teaming up in groups, giving high school students the skills and mindset to start real companies.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="References">References</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a
