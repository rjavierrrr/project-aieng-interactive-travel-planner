Association">NCAA</a>)</td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p><b>Coliseo Rub\xc3\xa9n Rodr\xc3\xadguez</b> (English: Ruben Rodr\xc3\xadguez Coliseum) is an <a href="/wiki/Indoor_arena" class="mw-redirect" title="Indoor arena">indoor sporting arena</a> in Puerto Rico. The coliseum was dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Rub%C3%A9n_Rodr%C3%ADguez_(basketball)" title="Rub\xc3\xa9n Rodr\xc3\xadguez (basketball)">Rub\xc3\xa9n Rodr\xc3\xadguez</a> (former player of the <a href="/wiki/Vaqueros_de_Bayam%C3%B3n" title="Vaqueros de Bayam\xc3\xb3n">Vaqueros de Bayam\xc3\xb3n</a>). It is located in <a href="/wiki/Bayam%C3%B3n,_Puerto_Rico" title="Bayam\xc3\xb3n, Puerto Rico">Bayam\xc3\xb3n, Puerto Rico</a>. It can accommodate up to 12,000 spectators and can be reached by the <a href="/wiki/Tren_Urbano" title="Tren Urbano">Tren Urbano</a> system from the <i>Deportivo</i> station.\n</p>\n<div
