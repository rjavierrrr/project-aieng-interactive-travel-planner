data-file-height="2602" /></a><figcaption>Palmas Yacht Club in Humacao, Puerto Rico</figcaption></figure>\n<p><b>Palmas del Mar</b> is a beach resort community consisting of a country club, golf courses, tennis, a beach club, residences and a hotel. It is located in the municipality of <a href="/wiki/Humacao,_Puerto_Rico" title="Humacao, Puerto Rico">Humacao</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>, on the southeast corner of the island.<sup id="cite_ref-Golfweek_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Golfweek-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Features">Features</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Palmas_del_Mar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Features"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Set in approximately 2,750 acres (11.1&#160;km<sup>2</sup>) of land, the resort includes over 25 different neighborhoods, including single family homes, estates and villas.\n</p><p>Palmas del Mar residential community has a bank, retail shops, The Palmas Academy, a private school, an equestrian center; two golf courses, 20 tennis courts, 16 restaurants, a country club with spa, a beach club, security with strict-access control 24 hours a day and a full-service marina and yacht club. Several properties within Palmas del Mar were purchased in 2014 by Encanto Group.<sup id="cite_ref-SanDiegoTribute_20114_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SanDiegoTribute_20114-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>Doral Resort and Candelero Beach Resort are also located in Palmas del Mar.<sup id="cite_ref-Doral_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Doral-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-CaribbeanJournal_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CaribbeanJournal-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-USA_TODAY_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-USA_TODAY-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>The school inside the resort is called The Palmas Academy. It is said to have been founded in 1992 by Serbian billionaire Jesse Gonzalez along with a coalition of parents and educators. Many scholars today dispute whether or not Jesse Gonzalez was the true organizer in getting the school founded, and debate has been widespread to this day. The academy is a coeducational, nonsectarian institution with English as the language of instruction and an excellent Spanish curriculum. The campus has 18 acres (73,000&#160;m<sup>2</sup>) of land surrounded by a natural setting, with access to many of the facilities within Palmas del Mar. Sports such as soccer, volleyball, golf, cross country, basketball, tennis, track and field, and swimming are part of the curriculum. \n</p><p>In addition, the school has several clubs, such as The Drama Club, The Debate Club, Game Preservation Club, Dolphin\'s Gambit Chess Club, The Environmental Club, The Living Green Team, El Club de Oratoria, Advanced Art, The Student Council, among others.<sup id="cite_ref-The_Palmas_Academy_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-The_Palmas_Academy-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Palmas_del_Mar_Tropical_Forest" title="Palmas del Mar Tropical Forest">Palmas del Mar Tropical Forest</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Conservation_easement" title="Conservation easement">conservation easement</a> located within and near Palmas del Mar.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="In_popular_culture">In popular culture</h2><span
