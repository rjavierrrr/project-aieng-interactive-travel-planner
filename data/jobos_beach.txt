01.jpg</figcaption></figure>\n<p><b>Jobos Beach</b> or <b>Playa Jobos</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Beach" title="Beach">beach</a> facing the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> located on the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Puerto_Rico_Highway_466&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Puerto Rico Highway 466 (page does not exist)">PR-466</a> street of <a href="/wiki/Isabela,_Puerto_Rico" title="Isabela, Puerto Rico">Isabela</a> in the northwest of <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> While popular, the beach is dangerous and signs warning tourists of dangerous currents were installed in early 2021.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>Jobos Beach is known as a world-class surf spot<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> and a good place to <a href="/wiki/Party" title="Party">party</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-NYT_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NYT-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In February 2013, the beach hosted the <a href="/wiki/Rip_Curl_Pro" title="Rip Curl Pro">Rip Curl Pro</a>, a world tour surfing competition.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  Jobos is also a popular beach for windsurfing.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Nearby, also in Isabela, is <span title="Spanish-language text"><i lang="es"><a href="/wiki/Pozo_de_Jacinto" title="Pozo de Jacinto">Pozo de Jacinto</a></i></span>, a pit cave steeped in folklore.<sup id="cite_ref-EV_Legend_2016_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-EV_Legend_2016-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>The area near Jobos Beach suffered heavy damage from <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Maria" title="Hurricane
