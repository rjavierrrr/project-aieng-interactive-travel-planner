stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Carolina,_Puerto_Rico" title="Carolina, Puerto Rico">Carolina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>.  It is currently used mostly for <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> games and is the home of <a href="/wiki/Gigantes_de_Carolina_(baseball)" title="Gigantes de Carolina (baseball)">Gigantes de Carolina</a>.  The stadium holds 12,500 people and was built in 2000.  It is named after former Puerto Rican baseball great and native of Carolina, <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Clemente" title="Roberto Clemente">Roberto Clemente</a>.\n</p><p>It hosted the <a href="/wiki/2007_Caribbean_Series" title="2007 Caribbean Series">2007 Caribbean Series</a>. From 2008 it is the home of the <a href="/wiki/Gigantes_de_Carolina_(football_club)" class="mw-redirect" title="Gigantes de Carolina (football club)">Gigantes de Carolina (football club)</a> who plays in the <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico_Soccer_League" title="Puerto Rico Soccer League">Puerto Rico Soccer League</a>.\n</p><p>It has been a venue for political campaign events,<sup id="cite_ref-1"
