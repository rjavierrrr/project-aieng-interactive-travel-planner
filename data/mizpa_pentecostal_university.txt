title="Spanish-language text"><i lang="es">Universidad Pentecostal Mizpa</i></span>) is a theological university<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> located in <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan, Puerto Rico</a>, led and administrated by the <a href="/wiki/Pentecostal_Church_of_God" title="Pentecostal Church of God">Pentecostal Church of God</a>, International Movement of Puerto Rico Region.\n</p><p>Founded in 1937<span class="noprint">&#32;(87&#160;years ago)</span><span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="bday dtstart published updated">1937</span>)</span>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Mizpa is the oldest Pentecostal theological school in Puerto Rico.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mizpa_Pentecostal_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>In 1937, the Juan L. Lugo founded the Mizpa Bible Institute. The first Pentecostal missionary, Longo established the institute in barrio <a href="/wiki/Santurce,_San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="Santurce, San Juan, Puerto Rico">Santurce</a> in the facilities of the Pentecostal Church of God, International Movement. On January 9, 1939, the first 12 students graduated.  The Institute later moved to barrio <a href="/wiki/P%C3%A1jaros,_Bayam%C3%B3n,_Puerto_Rico" title="P\xc3\xa1jaros, Bayam\xc3\xb3n, Puerto Rico">Pajaros</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bayam%C3%B3n,_Puerto_Rico" title="Bayam\xc3\xb3n, Puerto Rico">Bayamon</a>.\n</p><p>In 1956, Lugo move the institute to the barrio <a href="/wiki/Caimito,_San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="Caimito, San Juan, Puerto Rico">Caimito</a> in <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_Piedras,_Puerto_Rico" title="R\xc3\xado Piedras, Puerto Rico">Rio Piedras</a>, its current location. . Currently the college community is represented by more than 13 denominations sisters.\n</p><p>In 1985, the Pentecostal Seminary of Puerto Rico (SEPRI) and Mizpa Bible Institute merged under the name of Mizpa Pentecostal College. The new college offered university-level education with associates and bachelor\'s degrees.\n</p><p>In February 2001, received the status "Initial Accreditation" from the <a href="/wiki/Association_for_Biblical_Higher_Education" title="Association for Biblical Higher Education">Association for Biblical Higher Education</a> (ABHE).  In February 2006, the ABHE reaffirmed the accreditation of the College for the next ten years.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Programs">Programs</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mizpa_Pentecostal_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Programs"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Certificates">Certificates</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mizpa_Pentecostal_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Certificates"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>Mizpa offers certificate programs in 20 centers across the island\n</p>\n<ul><li>Chaplaincy</li>\n<li>Christian education</li>\n<li>Ministerial erts</li></ul>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Associate_degrees">Associate degrees</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mizpa_Pentecostal_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Associate degrees"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Catechesis" title="Catechesis">Christian education</a></li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Christian_counseling" title="Christian counseling">Christian counseling</a></li>\n<li>Pastoral theology</li>\n<li><a href="/wiki/Missiology" title="Missiology">Missiology</a></li></ul>\n<p>Mizpa offer courses in professional improvement to all pastors who are promoted by the Church of God Pentecostal, M.
