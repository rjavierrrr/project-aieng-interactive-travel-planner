href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i>T\xc3\xbaneles subterr\xc3\xa1neos de San Germ\xc3\xa1n</i>) is a vaulted brick storm sewer system built in 1835 underneath the urban center of <a href="/wiki/San_Germ%C3%A1n,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Germ\xc3\xa1n, Puerto Rico">San Germ\xc3\xa1n, Puerto Rico</a>.  The system is made of a central tunnel and several smaller side tunnels. The construction is of <a href="/wiki/Brick_and_rubble" class="mw-redirect" title="Brick and rubble">brick and rubble</a> with modern concrete repairs. The main part of the system covers the underground course of <a href="/w/index.php?title=Quebrada_Manzanares&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Quebrada Manzanares (page does not exist)">Quebrada Manzanares</a> from its headwaters to its resurgence as a surface stream, 842.53 metres (2,764.2&#160;ft) away.<sup id="cite_ref-nps_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nps-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>  The vaulted brick branches, most of them abandoned and sealed, date mostly from before 1910. The tunnel sections built up to 1918 are listed on the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a> as <b>Alcantarilla Pluvial sobre la Quebrada Manzanares</b>.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="See_also">See also</h2><span
