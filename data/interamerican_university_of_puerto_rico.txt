data-file-width="1521" data-file-height="577" /></a></span></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Inter American University of Puerto Rico</b> (Spanish: <i>Universidad Interamericana de Puerto Rico</i>; often abbreviated to <i>UIPR</i> or <i>Inter</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Private_university" title="Private university">private</a> <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christian</a> university with its main campus in <a href="/wiki/San_Germ%C3%A1n,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Germ\xc3\xa1n, Puerto Rico">San Germ\xc3\xa1n, Puerto Rico</a>.  It also has campuses in <a href="/wiki/Aguadilla" class="mw-redirect" title="Aguadilla">Aguadilla</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arecibo" class="mw-redirect" title="Arecibo">Arecibo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Barranquitas" class="mw-redirect" title="Barranquitas">Barranquitas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bayam%C3%B3n" class="mw-redirect" title="Bayam\xc3\xb3n">Bayam\xc3\xb3n</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fajardo" class="mw-redirect" title="Fajardo">Fajardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guayama" class="mw-redirect" title="Guayama">Guayama</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ponce,_Puerto_Rico" title="Ponce, Puerto Rico">Ponce</a>, and <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan</a>. The university also has three professional schools: <a href="/wiki/Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico,_School_of_Optometry" title="Interamerican University of Puerto Rico, School of Optometry">School of Optometry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico_School_of_Law" title="Interamerican University of Puerto Rico School of Law">School of Law</a>, and the School of Aeronautics. The Inter offers academic programs in 11 teaching units.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> It was founded in San Germ\xc3\xa1n in 1912. The San Germ\xc3\xa1n campus is also the home to the Inter American School, a private co-educational college-preparatory school.\n</p>\n<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The Inter American University was founded as Polytechnic Institute of Puerto Rico in 1912 by Rev. John Will Harris and his brother Clarence Harris. It was founded as an elementary and high school in the Lomas de Santa Marta sector of the town of San Germ\xc3\xa1n in land now occupied by the university\'s San Germ\xc3\xa1n Campus. In 1927, the first high school class graduation took place. In 1944, the institute was accredited by the <a href="/wiki/Middle_States_Association_of_Colleges_and_Secondary_Schools" class="mw-redirect" title="Middle States Association of Colleges and Secondary Schools">Middle States Association of Colleges and Secondary Schools</a>, thus becoming the first liberal arts college to receive such accreditation in Puerto Rico, as well as the first outside the continental United States. This accreditation has been maintained over the years.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Governance">Governance</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Interamerican_University_of_Puerto_Rico&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Governance"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The governing body of the Inter American University of Puerto Rico is its self-perpetuating Board of Trustees.
