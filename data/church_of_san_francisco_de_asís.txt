Mayag\xc3\xbcez</a></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>Church of San Francisco de As\xc3\xads de la Aguada</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i>Iglesia San Francisco de As\xc3\xads de la Aguada</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Parish_(Catholic_Church)" title="Parish (Catholic Church)">parish</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Church" title="Catholic Church">Catholic Church</a> in <a href="/wiki/Aguada,_Puerto_Rico" title="Aguada, Puerto Rico">Aguada, Puerto Rico</a>. It is located on the eastern end of the <i>Plaza de Aguada</i> (the main town square) in <a href="/wiki/Aguada_barrio-pueblo" title="Aguada barrio-pueblo">downtown Aguada</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> \n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Church_of_San_Francisco_de_As%C3%ADs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>\n<p>The Parish of Aguada was officially established in 1692 and it has belonged to four different dioceses throughout its history. Although the current church dates to the early 20th century, different wooden church buildings have occupied the space. The previous structure was destroyed during the <a href="/wiki/1918_San_Ferm%C3%ADn_earthquake" title="1918 San Ferm\xc3\xadn earthquake">1918 Puerto Rico earthquake</a>, The church was designed by architects Antonio Mart\xc3\xadnez and Jos\xc3\xa9 Lazaro, and construction took place between the years 1919 and 1926.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The church is currently undergoing extensive renovations.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2
