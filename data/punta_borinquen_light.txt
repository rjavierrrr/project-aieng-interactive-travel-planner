href="/wiki/Ramey_Air_Force_Base" title="Ramey Air Force Base">Ramey Air Force Base</a> in <a href="/wiki/Aguadilla,_Puerto_Rico" title="Aguadilla, Puerto Rico">Aguadilla, Puerto Rico</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> The station was established in 1889 by the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish government</a>. With the opening of the <a href="/wiki/Panama_Canal" title="Panama Canal">Panama Canal</a> in 1914, the lighthouse would become "the most important aid to navigation on the route from Europe to Panama".<sup id="cite_ref-Friends_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Friends-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> In 1917, the U.S Congress provided funding for a new lighthouse in higher ground.\n</p><p>But before construction began on the new structure, the original lighthouse was severely damaged by the <a href="/wiki/1918_San_Ferm%C3%ADn_earthquake" title="1918 San Ferm\xc3\xadn earthquake">1918 earthquake</a> that struck the west part of the island.<sup id="cite_ref-Record_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Record-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> Construction on the new lighthouse was completed in 1922. The light is active aid to navigation and is a housing facility  for the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Coast_Guard" title="United States Coast Guard">United States Coast Guard</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6"
