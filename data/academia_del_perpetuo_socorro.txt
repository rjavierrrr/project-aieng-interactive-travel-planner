Our Lady of Perpetual Help") was founded in 1921 as a Catholic parochial school of the Perpetuo Socorro Parish at the Archdiocese of <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan, Puerto Rico</a>. The school is located in <a href="/wiki/Miramar,_Puerto_Rico" class="mw-redirect" title="Miramar, Puerto Rico">Miramar</a> in <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>\'s capital city of <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan</a>. To this end, the school works to create a Catholic academic community in which faith, knowledge, and recreation are shared in a spirit of freedom, love, and creativity. The school, however, does not emphasize religion over other subjects, accepts students of all faiths and does not impose religious requirements such as baptism, confirmation or <a href="/wiki/Church_attendance" title="Church attendance">church attendance</a> as a condition for continued attendance.\n</p>\n<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Motto">Motto</h2><span
